My studies

Since I was a little girl, I love getting excellent scores in my school. Thanks God, I have always succeed on my studies, and it has been thanks to him and to my effort, the reason why I am going to graduate soon from high school.

There have been 3 years full of challenges, tears and discouragement; because in the mornings I have had to study in my school and in the afternoons I have had to study in Centro ¡Supérate! ADOC. Now I can say that every little and big effort I have done these last few years have been worth it.
